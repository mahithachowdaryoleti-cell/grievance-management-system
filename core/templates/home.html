Welcome

Welcome to the College Grievance Management System (GMS). This portal provides a secure and transparent platform for students, faculty, and staff to register complaints, track their status, and ensure timely resolution by the concerned departments. The system promotes accountability, efficiency, and better communication within the institution.

How the System Works

  1. User logs into the Grievance Management System.
  2. The user submits a complaint with required details and attachments.
  3. The complaint is automatically assigned to the concerned department.
  4. Department staff review and resolve the issue.
  5. The user receives updates and can track the complaint status.
  6. After resolution, the user can provide feedback on the service.

Complaint Categories

  • Infrastructure Issues (classrooms, electricity, water supply)
  • Academic Concerns (faculty, timetable, examinations)
  • Hostel & Accommodation Issues
  • Library Services
  • IT & Network Support (WiFi, portal access)
  • Administrative Services
  • Cafeteria / Canteen Services

User Roles

  • Student / User – Submit complaints and track their resolution status.
  • Department Staff – Review, process, and resolve assigned grievances.
  • System Administrator – Manage users, departments, reports, and system operations.

Why Use This System?

  • Fast and transparent grievance resolution
  • Secure digital complaint registration
  • Real-time complaint tracking
  • Role-based access control
  • Centralized complaint management dashboard
  • Improved communication between students and administration
