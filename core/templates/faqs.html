Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

📌 General Questions

Q1. What is the Grievance Management System (GMS)?
It is an online platform that allows students and staff to register complaints, track their status, and receive timely resolutions.

Q2. Who can use this system?
All registered students, faculty members, and administrative staff of the institution can use this system.

Q3. What types of complaints can be submitted?
Complaints related to academics, hostel, infrastructure, IT services, and administration can be submitted.

📌 Complaint Process

Q4. How do I file a complaint?
Go to the “File Complaint” section, fill in the details, select the category, and submit the form.

Q5. Can I track my complaint status?
Yes, you can track the status under the “My Complaints” section.

Q6. How long does it take to resolve a complaint?
Resolution time depends on the nature of the complaint, but authorities aim to resolve issues promptly.

Q7. Can I edit or delete my complaint after submission?
No, once submitted, complaints cannot be edited. You can contact support for further assistance.

📌 Account & Login

Q8. What should I do if I forget my password?
Use the “Reset Password” option to set a new password.

Q9. Can I change my profile details?
Yes, you can update your profile information in the Profile section.

Q10. Why am I unable to log in?
Ensure your credentials are correct. If the issue persists, contact support.

📌 Technical Issues

Q11. What if the system is not loading properly?
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

Q12. Which browsers are supported?
The system works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Edge, and Firefox.

Q13. Can I use this system on mobile?
Yes, the system is accessible on mobile devices.

📌 Contact & Support

Q14. Who should I contact for urgent issues?
You can contact the respective department or system administrator.

Q15. How will I be notified about updates?
You will receive notifications on your dashboard when the complaint status changes.
